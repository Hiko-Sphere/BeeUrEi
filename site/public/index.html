核心能力What it does

为「出行」与「感知」而生Built for moving through the world

每一项都为日常出行设计：避开眼前的障碍，听见周围的世界，需要时一键找人帮你看一眼。Every capability is built for everyday life — steer clear of what’s ahead, hear the world around you, and reach a real person when you need one.

端侧实时避障Real-time obstacle avoidance

打开就在运行——主屏顶部那行「正前方通畅」就是它的实时状态。用摄像头与 LiDAR/深度感知前方，说出距离与「钟面方向」，让你自己做决定。It runs the moment you open the app — that “path clear ahead” line at the top of the home screen is its live status. Using the camera and LiDAR/depth, it senses what’s ahead and speaks the distance and a clock-face direction, so you decide.

  • 接近声呐：越近，提示音越急（像倒车雷达）Proximity sonar: beeps speed up as you get closer, like parking sensors
  • 空间音方向提示（搭配 AirPods）Spatial-audio direction cues with AirPods

“前方两点钟方向，1.5 米。”“2 o’clock ahead, 1.5 metres.” 这就是蜂之眼说话的方式。This is how BeeUrEi speaks.

看一看：朗读文字、识物、辨色See for me: read text, identify objects & colours

从主屏点「看一看」，一个九宫格把摄像头变成多面手：朗读文字与整页、识别颜色、扫码、辨纸币、找东西、识别公交、感知「周围的人」与光线——这些都在本机完成；另有可选的云端「AI 描述」，对眼前画面给出更丰富的自然语言描述，并能对同一张图**连续追问**（一问接一问，模型带着上下文回答）。Tap “See for me” on the home screen and a single grid turns the camera into an all-rounder: read text or a whole page, identify colours, scan codes, recognise banknotes, find things, read bus numbers, and sense people nearby and ambient light — all of these on-device; plus an optional cloud “AI Describe” for a richer scene description and conversational Q&A — keep asking follow-up questions about the same photo and it answers in context.

  • 常用识别一屏直达，按钮大而清晰Common tools one tap away, large and clear
  • 「前方有什么」一句话汇总眼前场景“What’s ahead” sums up the scene in one sentence
  • 识别历史：读过、扫过的内容存在本机，随时回放重听、按关键词搜索（复核刚读的信件、再确认扫过的商品）——全程端侧，锁屏即不可读，可一键清空Read history: what you read or scan is saved on-device to replay or search by keyword (revisit a letter you just read, re-check a product you scanned) — fully on-device, locked when your phone is, clearable anytime

步行 · 公交导航与原路返回Walking & transit navigation, retrace

输入目的地，跟着语音与「钟面方向」步行；海外用 Apple 地图、中国大陆用高德。进陌生地方先「开始记路」，要回去时「原路返回」，跟着提示音走回出发点。Enter a destination and walk by voice and clock-face directions — Apple Maps abroad, AMap in mainland China. Tap “Start a breadcrumb” before somewhere unfamiliar, then “Retrace” to follow audio cues back to where you started.

  • 过城坐公交/地铁：说「坐公交去X」即读出整条换乘方案——换几次车、票价多少、每段坐哪条线、往哪个方向、从哪个口进出、坐几站下车；深夜常规车收班后，自动把夜班车也算进来，不至于查了个已停运的方案空等Public transit across town: say “take transit to X” to hear the whole route — number of transfers, fare, and for each leg the line, direction, station entrance/exit, and how many stops to your stop; late at night, after regular lines stop, it automatically includes night buses so you're not sent to wait for one that isn't running
  • 出门前可「预览路线」先试听一遍Preview the route by ear before you set off
  • 常用目的地一键直达Saved places in one tap

一键远程协助One-tap remote assistance

主屏顶部的「求助」直接呼叫你选定的亲友或志愿者，发起实时视频通话（WebRTC + CallKit），让对方帮你看一眼。The “Get help” button at the top of the home screen calls a family member or volunteer you’ve chosen — a live video call (WebRTC + CallKit) so a real person can take a look.

  • 求助时画面默认不发送，按住「显示画面」对方才看得到Your camera isn’t shared by default — only while you hold “Show My Camera”
  • 来电用系统级 CallKit 呈现，像普通电话一样接听Calls ring through CallKit, answered like any phone call
  • 录制须双方明确同意，加密保存、到期自动删除Recording needs both parties’ consent; stored encrypted, auto-deleted on expiry

和亲友、协助者保持联系Stay close to family and helpers

内置消息：文字、语音、图片、位置与简短视频消息，还能建群。无障碍优先，随时找人，也随时被找到。Built-in messaging — text, voice, photos, location and short video notes, plus group chats. Accessibility-first, so reaching someone (or being reached) is always easy.

  • 单聊与群聊，消息可朗读One-to-one and group chats, messages read aloud
  • 语音、图片、位置与简短视频消息Voice, photos, location and short video notes

按你的习惯来Make it yours

设置以盲人优先重排：最重要的安全开关置顶，语音语速、详略、简短播报一目了然，次要项收进「更多设置」。Settings put blind users first — the most important safety switches sit at the top; speech rate, verbosity and concise mode are right there, with secondary options tucked into “More settings”.

  • VoiceOver 全程优先，文字大小跟随系统VoiceOver-first throughout; text scales with the system

它还能做的It also

知道你身在何处Always know where you are

「我在哪」播报当前位置、朝向与最近的地标；「周围有什么」按时钟方位环顾四周；「前方有什么」汇总正前方的场景；还能语音直接找「最近的厕所 / 药店」，找到后再说一句「带我去那里」，就用它的精确位置一路把你导航过去——不必自己再报一遍地名。“Where am I” speaks your location, heading and nearest landmarks; “What’s around” scans your surroundings by clock direction; “What’s ahead” sums up the scene in front of you; and you can voice-search for the nearest restroom or pharmacy — then just say “take me there” and it navigates you to that exact spot, no need to name the place again.

天气一问便知Weather at a word

出门前一句话播报当前天气，帮你决定怎么穿、要不要带伞。A one-line spoken weather report before you head out — what to wear, whether to take an umbrella.

摔倒报警 · 紧急呼叫Fall alerts · emergency

检测疑似摔倒或剧烈撞击，未及时取消则通知家人；一键紧急呼叫并尽力附带粗略位置。还能提前发一条测试告警，看看你的联系人到底收不收得到——万一有人没装 App 或没开通知，趁没出事就先修好，而不是真求助时才发现没人收到。Detects a suspected fall or impact and notifies family if not cancelled; one-tap emergency call with a best-effort coarse location. You can also send a test alert ahead of time to see whether your contacts actually receive it — so if someone hasn't installed the app or turned on notifications, you fix it before an emergency, not during one.

安全报到 · 报平安Safety check-in

独自出门前设一个时限，到点没「报平安」会自动把情况和位置通知你的紧急联系人；回来一句「报平安」即可结束，也支持每日固定时刻的自动报到。Set a timer before heading out alone — if you don’t check in, your emergency contacts are alerted with your location. Saying “I’m safe” ends it, and a daily automatic check-in is available too.

紧急医疗信息Emergency medical info

填好血型、过敏、正在用的药和慢性病，遇险时只有你已接受的紧急联系人能看到，帮医护更快做对判断；信息加密存储，绝不公开、也不会进推送内容。太久没更新还会提醒你复核，免得施救者拿到过时信息。Fill in your blood type, allergies, current medications and conditions — visible only to your accepted emergency contacts when you need help, so responders can act faster and safer. It’s encrypted at rest, never public and never inside a notification. If it goes stale, you’re reminded to review it so no one acts on outdated info.

认钱 · 点钞 · 读保质期Money, cash counting & expiry dates

认出纸币面额、逐张点钞报运行总额；拍一下包装读出保质期与生产日期，扫条码还能报出商品名、过敏原、配料表、营养标注，以及热量与四大营养素克数——每 100 克的千卡、碳水、蛋白质、脂肪，糖尿病算胰岛素要的正是碳水克数（含量档只给「高糖」给不了这个数）；查明胶、忌口成分、数卡路里控糖控重，或「这到底用什么做的」。Identifies banknotes and counts a stack aloud with a running total; snaps packaging to read expiry and production dates, and scans barcodes for product names, allergens, the full ingredients list, nutrition labels, and calories plus macronutrient grams — kcal, carbs, protein and fat per 100 g, and the carb grams are exactly what diabetics count for insulin (the high/low labels can't give that number) — check for gelatin, a specific ingredient, count calories for diet or diabetes, or “what is this actually made of”.

亲友替你画的路线Routes drawn by family

亲友在网页地图上替你踩好常走路线（家到菜场），你沿着提示音一步步走；每条路线都注明是谁画的，改动或删除都会提醒你。Family draw your familiar routes on a web map (home to the market); you follow audio beacons point by point. Every route says who drew it, and you’re notified when one changes or is removed.

隐私即安全Privacy is safety

看见世界，不被世界窥看See the world without being watched

  • 视觉 AI 默认在本机运行Vision AI runs on-device by default避障与识别在本机完成，画面不离开手机；仅当你主动使用「AI 描述」时，那一张图才经我们自己的服务器发往云端视觉模型——不留存、不用于训练。Obstacle avoidance and recognition run on your phone and frames stay there.   • 只有你主动发起协助时才共享视频Video is shared only when you start assistance而且只共享给你亲自选择的人。And only with the person you choose.

  • 我们不做的事What we never do不识别人脸、不出售数据、不收集精确位置历史、不做广告画像、无第三方追踪。No face identification, no data selling, no precise-location history, no ad profiling, no third-party trackers.

无障碍承诺Accessibility commitment

可以完全凭听觉与触觉使用Fully usable by ear and touch

蜂之眼以 VoiceOver 优先设计，无需视力即可操作；界面与实时引导均为简体中文 / English 双语，文字大小跟随系统设置，关键安全功能默认开启。这个官网本身也遵循 WCAG 2.2 标准——因为为视障人士做的产品，连介绍它的网页都应当人人可用。BeeUrEi is built VoiceOver-first and is fully operable without sight. Its interface and real-time guidance are bilingual (简体中文 / English), text scales with your system settings, and the key safety features are on by default. This website itself follows WCAG 2.2 — because a product made for blind people should have a page everyone can use, too.

视障用户、协助者与家人各有专属体验，连成可以彼此照应的网络。登录方式由你挑，无障碍优先。Blind users, helpers and family each get their own experience, woven into a network that looks out for one another. Pick whatever sign-in suits you — accessibility first.

重要安全声明An important safety note

BeeUrEi 蜂之眼是一款增强感知的辅助工具，而非安全设备。它不能替代白手杖、导盲犬或定向行走（O&M）训练，也无法检测出每一个障碍物。请始终把它作为现有出行方法的补充，而非替代。BeeUrEi is a perception-enhancing assistive tool, not a safety device. It does not replace a white cane, a guide dog, or orientation & mobility training, and it cannot detect every obstacle. Always use it to complement your existing mobility methods — never to replace them.

常见问题FAQ

你可能想知道You might be wondering

需要哪种 iPhone？Which iPhone do I need?
实时避障依赖 LiDAR，需要带激光雷达的 iPhone（iPhone 12 Pro 及更新的 Pro 机型）。其余功能（识别、远程协助、消息等）在更多机型上也可使用。Real-time obstacle avoidance relies on LiDAR, so it needs a LiDAR-equipped iPhone (iPhone 12 Pro or newer Pro models). Other features — recognition, remote assistance, messaging — work on more devices.
需要联网吗？Does it need the internet?
避障与识别在本机离线运行，不需要联网。远程协助、消息与紧急通知是联网功能。Obstacle avoidance and recognition run on-device and work offline. Remote assistance, messaging and emergency notifications use the network.
我的摄像头画面会被上传吗？Are my camera frames uploaded?
默认不会——避障与识别都在本机完成，画面不离开手机。只有两种由你主动发起的情况会传出画面：① 向你选定的人发起远程协助并按住「显示画面」；② 使用「AI 描述」或对图片提问时，仅那一张图经我们的服务器发往云端视觉模型（不留存、不用于训练）。Not by default — obstacle avoidance and recognition run on your phone and frames stay there. Frames leave only in two cases you start yourself: (1) starting remote assistance with someone you choose and holding “Show My Camera”; (2) using AI Describe or asking about a photo, where that single image goes via our server to a cloud vision model (never stored or used for training).
它能替代白手杖或导盲犬吗？Can it replace a cane or guide dog?
不能。它是增强感知的辅助工具，不是安全设备，无法检测出每一个障碍。请始终保留并优先使用白手杖、导盲犬与定向行走训练。No. It is a perception-enhancing aid, not a safety device, and cannot detect every obstacle. Always keep and prioritise your white cane, guide dog and O&M training.
多少钱？什么时候上架？What does it cost? When is it out?
蜂之眼采用 PolyForm 非商用许可，面向其服务的视障用户不收费。iPhone 版尚未上架 App Store，正在准备中；协助者与家人的网页版现已可用。BeeUrEi uses a PolyForm Noncommercial licence and is not charged to the blind users it serves. The iPhone app isn’t on the App Store yet — meanwhile the helper & family web app is live.

独立，是可以被技术守护的Independence, quietly supported by technology

iPhone 版尚未上架 App Store；协助者与家人的网页版现已可用——通话、消息、位置与紧急告警都在浏览器里就绪。如果你认同这件事，欢迎一起把它做得更好。The iPhone app isn’t on the App Store yet — but the helper & family web app is live today: calls, messages, location and emergency alerts, right in the browser.
