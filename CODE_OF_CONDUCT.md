# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

## Our pledge

BeeUrEi exists to help blind and low-vision people. In that spirit, we pledge to
make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of disability, age, body, ethnicity, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- using welcoming and inclusive language;
- being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences;
- gracefully accepting constructive feedback;
- prioritizing accessibility and the safety of real users in every discussion.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, derogatory comments, personal
attacks, and publishing others' private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported privately by email
to **<beeurei@163.com>**. All reports will be reviewed and handled with
discretion.

---

## 我们的承诺

BeeUrEi 旨在帮助视障与低视力人士。秉持这一初心，我们承诺让每个人都能在本项目中获得
受尊重、无骚扰的参与体验，无论其残障状况、年龄、体型、族裔、性别认同与表达、经验水平、
国籍、外貌、种族、宗教或性取向如何。

## 我们的准则

有助于营造积极环境的行为示例：

- 使用友善、包容的语言；
- 尊重不同的观点与经历；
- 大方接受建设性反馈；
- 在每一次讨论中，优先考虑无障碍与真实用户的安全。

不可接受的行为包括骚扰、贬损性言论、人身攻击，以及未经许可公开他人隐私信息。

## 执行

如遇辱骂或不可接受的行为，可通过邮件**私下**报告至 **<beeurei@163.com>**。所有报告
都将被审阅并酌情妥善处理。
