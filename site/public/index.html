让 iPhone 摄像头，
成为你前行的眼睛。 Let an iPhone camera
become eyes that walk with you.

实时避障、场景识别、远程协助与紧急呼叫——把感知交还给你，把独立留给你。 Real-time obstacle avoidance, scene understanding, live human assistance and emergency calls — perception returned to you, independence kept by you.

了解功能Explore the features 隐私如何保护How privacy works

所有视觉 AI 都在你的设备本地运行，画面默认不离开手机。 All vision AI runs on your device. Camera frames don’t leave the phone by default.

核心能力What it does

一套围绕「出行」与「感知」的完整能力A complete toolkit for moving through the world

每一项都为日常出行设计：从避开眼前的障碍，到读出一段文字、辨认一张纸币，再到一键找到信任的人帮你看一眼。Each capability is built for everyday life — from steering clear of what’s ahead, to reading text aloud and identifying a banknote, to a one-tap video call with someone you trust.

端侧实时避障Real-time obstacle avoidance

用摄像头与 LiDAR/深度感知障碍物，说出距离与时钟方向，例如「前方两点钟方向，1.5 米」。Uses the camera and LiDAR/depth to sense obstacles and speak the distance and clock-face direction — e.g. “2 o’clock ahead, 1.5 metres.”

  • 可选接近声呐：越近，提示音越急促（类似倒车雷达）Optional proximity sonar: beeps speed up as you get closer, like parking sensors
  • 可选空间音频方向提示（搭配 AirPods）Optional spatial-audio direction cues with AirPods

步行导航Walking navigation 开发中In development

为行人量身的逐向语音导航：海外使用 Apple 地图，中国大陆使用持牌地图服务商。Pedestrian turn-by-turn guidance: Apple Maps overseas, and a licensed map provider in mainland China.

场景识别Scene & object understanding

全部在设备本地完成：识别物体、朗读文字、辨认纸币、扫描条码、识别颜色、寻找物品、感知「附近有人」与环境光线。All on-device: recognise objects, read text aloud, identify banknotes, scan barcodes, detect colours, find-my-things, sense “people nearby” and ambient light.

远程协助Remote assistance

一键向你选定的帮手、家人或志愿者发起实时视频通话（WebRTC + CallKit）。如需录制，必须经双方明确同意。One tap starts a live video call with a helper, family member or volunteer you choose (WebRTC + CallKit). Recording requires both parties’ explicit consent.

摔倒 / 撞击自动报警Fall & impact alerts

检测疑似摔倒或剧烈撞击，若未在限时内取消，自动通知已关联的家人。Detects a suspected fall or severe impact; if you don’t cancel in time, it notifies your linked family.

紧急呼叫家人Emergency call to family

紧急情况下一键呼叫家人，并尽力附带一个粗略位置。One tap calls family in an emergency, attaching a best-effort coarse location.

聊天 · 群聊 · 视频消息Messaging, group chat & video messages

与帮手和家人保持联系：文字聊天、群聊，以及简短的视频消息。Stay in touch with helpers and family: text chat, group chats and short video messages.

隐私优先Privacy-first

看见世界，不被世界窥看See the world without being watched

隐私不是附加项，而是这款应用的底层设计。所有视觉 AI 都在你的 iPhone 本地完成，画面默认不离开手机。 Privacy isn’t an add-on here — it’s the foundation. All vision AI runs locally on your iPhone, and camera frames don’t leave the phone by default.

  • 视觉 AI 全部在设备本地运行All vision AI runs on-device摄像头画面默认不离开你的手机。Camera frames stay on your phone by default.
  • 只有你主动发起协助时才共享视频Video is shared only when you start assistance而且只共享给你亲自选择的人。And only with the person you choose.
  • 不做人脸身份识别No face identification不出售数据、不记录精确位置历史、不做广告画像。No data selling, no precise-location history, no ad profiling.
  • 后端、信令与 TURN 全部自托管Self-hosted backend, signaling & TURN数据通路掌握在自己手里。The whole data path is run by us, not third parties.

你始终掌握主动权You stay in control

  • 视频协助由你发起、向你选定的人，随时可结束。Video assistance is started by you, to a person you pick, and can end any time.
  • 录制默认关闭，须通话双方明确同意，并加密保存、到期自动删除。Recording is off by default, needs both parties’ explicit consent, and is stored encrypted then auto-deleted on expiry.
  • 紧急呼叫只附带尽力而为的粗略位置。Emergency calls attach only a best-effort coarse location.
  • 没有第三方分析、追踪或广告 SDK。No third-party analytics, trackers or ad SDKs.

为彼此而建Built for each other

三种角色，一张互助的网Three roles, one circle of support

视障用户、帮手与家人各有专属体验，连成一张可以彼此照应的网络。Blind and low-vision users, helpers and family each get an experience of their own, woven into a network that looks out for one another.

视障 / 低视力用户Blind / low-vision user

把摄像头变成感知工具：避障、识物、读字、求助，一切以语音为先。Turns the camera into a sensing tool: avoid, identify, read and ask for help — voice-first throughout.

帮手 / 志愿者Helper / volunteer

接到视频请求时伸出援手——看一眼、读一句、指个方向。Lend a hand on a video request — take a look, read a line, point the way.

家人Family

保持联系并接收摔倒报警与紧急呼叫，安心不打扰。Stay connected and receive fall alerts and emergency calls — peace of mind, without hovering.

重要安全声明An important safety note

BeeUrEi 蜂之眼是一款增强感知的辅助工具，而非安全设备。它不能替代白手杖、导盲犬或定向行走（O&M）训练，也无法检测出每一个障碍物。请始终把它作为现有出行方法的补充，而不是替代。 BeeUrEi is a perception-enhancing assistive tool, not a safety device. It does not replace a white cane, a guide dog, or orientation & mobility training, and it cannot detect every obstacle. Always use it to complement your existing mobility methods — never to replace them.
