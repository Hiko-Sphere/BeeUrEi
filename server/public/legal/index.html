Privacy Policy

We treat privacy as part of safety. Core principle: your camera frames do not leave your phone by default; video is shared only when you actively start remote assistance with someone you choose.

1. On-device processing — All vision AI (obstacle avoidance, object/text/banknote/barcode/color recognition, find-my-things, "people nearby", light detection) runs locally on your iPhone. Camera and depth data are used only for local real-time analysis; never uploaded, stored, or used for training.
2. Data we process — Account (username, display name, avatar; optional email/phone/Apple ID for sign-in, recovery, contact lookup); relationships & communication (family/helper links, chat messages, call-routing metadata); push tokens; device language; optional coarse (province/city/district) location attached to help requests; optional crash diagnostics.
3. What we do NOT collect — We do not sell data, collect precise location history, build ad profiles, or identify people / store faces.
4. Third parties — Sign in with Apple; Apple Maps (overseas) / a licensed map provider (mainland China, proxied via our backend); Open-Meteo weather (reduced-precision coordinates only); Apple APNs push. Backend, signaling, and TURN are self-hosted.
5. Recording — Off by default; requires both parties' explicit consent; encrypted and auto-deleted on expiry.
6. Your rights — View/edit your account, remove links, or permanently delete your account anytime in the app.
7. Children — Not directed to children under 13; no knowing collection of their data.
8. Security — TLS in transit; salted-hashed passwords; file protection for on-device sensitive data.
9. Changes — Version and effective date updated here; material changes prompted in-app.
10. Contact — Hiko Sphere.

隐私政策（简体中文）

1. 端侧处理 —— 避障、识别物体/文字/纸币/扫码/颜色、找物、"周围的人"、光线探测等所有视觉 AI 都在本机完成；相机与深度数据仅用于本地实时分析，不上传、不留存、不用于训练。

4. 第三方 —— Sign in with Apple；Apple 地图（海外）/ 持牌图商（中国大陆，经后端转发）；Open-Meteo 天气（仅降精度坐标）；Apple APNs。后端/信令/TURN 均自托管。
