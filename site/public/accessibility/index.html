无障碍声明Accessibility Statement

版本 1.0 · 更新日期 2026-07-03 · 适用于 BeeUrEi iOS App、官网与协助者网页端Version 1.0 · Updated 2026-07-03 · Covers the BeeUrEi iOS app, this website and the helper web app

我们的承诺

BeeUrEi（蜂之眼）是一款为视障与低视力人士打造的产品——无障碍不是它的附加项，而是它存在的理由。我们按「盲人优先」设计每一条主要使用路径，并把无障碍回归检查作为每次发版的必经工序。

符合性状态

我们对照下列标准设计与自测，并持续改进：

  • iOS App：按 Apple 无障碍规范设计。注册、登录、避障、导航、看一看（识别）、远程协助通话、聊天、紧急求助与设置等核心流程均可在 VoiceOver 下完整完成；对未开启 VoiceOver 的盲人用户，App 以自带语音播报（TTS）作为并行通道，关键状态与错误都会被念出，绝不将原始错误代码念给用户。
  • 官网（本站）与协助者网页端：以 WCAG 2.2 AA 为目标（部分符合，见「已知局限」）。支持键盘操作、读屏软件、深浅色主题与中英双语。

「部分符合」的含义：绝大多数内容符合上述标准，但仍存在下列已知例外。

无障碍特性（节选）

  • App 内语音双通道：VoiceOver 用户走系统公告；未开 VoiceOver 时用 App 自带 TTS 念出，两者由统一的语音总线仲裁，避障警告永远最优先。
  • 通话内实时文字（RTT）：音视频通话中可并行收发文字并被自动读出，服务于嘈杂环境与听障-视障重合用户。
  • 大按钮、高对比度界面、防误触二次确认（挂断/静音）、VoiceOver 魔法轻点挂断。
  • 协助者网页端：焦点管理完整的对话框、aria-live 实时通知、键盘可达的关键操作。
  • 全站中英双语，App 播报语言可切换。

已知局限

  • 避障与导航的方向信息主要以语音与空间音提示音呈现；转向、到达与危险等级已有可区分的振动编码作为互补通道，但完整的纯振动导航（不依赖任何声音）仍在完善中。
  • 协助者网页端的地图视图（实时位置）以视觉呈现为主；同页提供文字化的位置摘要，但地图本身的读屏体验有限。
  • 录音回放使用浏览器原生播放器控件，其无障碍体验取决于所用浏览器。
  • 管理后台面向运营人员，尚未按 WCAG 2.2 AA 完整审计。

监督与投诉

依据欧盟《无障碍法案》（European Accessibility Act）等适用法规，如你认为我们未充分回应你的无障碍诉求，你有权向所在国家/地区的市场监督或无障碍执法机构投诉。

本声明的制定方法

本声明基于我们对产品的自我评估：核心流程的 VoiceOver 全程实测、自动化无障碍回归测试（语音播报文案、焦点管理、aria 属性等均有测试锁定），以及针对视障使用场景的持续对抗审查。声明将随每次重大版本更新复核。

Our commitment

BeeUrEi is built for blind and low-vision users — accessibility is not an add-on, it is the reason this product exists. Every primary flow is designed blind-first, and accessibility regression checks are part of every release.

Conformance status

  • iOS app: designed to Apple's accessibility guidelines. Registration, sign-in, obstacle avoidance, navigation, recognition, remote-assist calls, chat, emergency and settings are fully operable with VoiceOver. For blind users who don't run VoiceOver, the app speaks key states and errors through its own TTS channel — raw error codes are never read out.
  • This website and the helper web app: target WCAG 2.2 AA (partially conformant — see known limitations). Keyboard operable, screen-reader friendly, light/dark themes, bilingual.

"Partially conformant" means most content conforms, with the known exceptions listed below.

Accessibility features (highlights)

  • Dual voice channels in-app: system announcements under VoiceOver, first-party TTS otherwise, arbitrated on one speech bus where obstacle warnings always win.
  • Real-time text (RTT) during calls: text runs alongside audio/video and is read aloud — for noisy places and deaf-blind users.
  • Large touch targets, high contrast, confirmation guards against accidental hang-up/mute, magic-tap to end calls.
  • Helper web app: dialogs with full focus management, aria-live notifications, keyboard-reachable actions.
  • Bilingual UI (Chinese/English) across app and web.

Known limitations

  • Direction from obstacle avoidance and navigation is conveyed mainly through speech and spatial audio; turns, arrival and danger levels also have distinct vibration patterns as a complementary channel, but full sound-free vibration-only navigation is still being refined.
  • The live-location map in the helper web app is primarily visual; a text summary is provided, but the map itself has limited screen-reader support.
  • Recording playback uses the browser's native player; its accessibility depends on the browser.
  • The admin console targets operations staff and has not been fully audited against WCAG 2.2 AA.

Feedback and contact

Enforcement

Under the European Accessibility Act and other applicable law, if you believe we have not adequately addressed your accessibility needs, you may lodge a complaint with the market-surveillance or accessibility-enforcement authority in your country.

How this statement was prepared

This statement is based on self-assessment: end-to-end VoiceOver testing of core flows, automated accessibility regression tests (spoken strings, focus management and ARIA attributes are locked by tests), and ongoing adversarial reviews focused on blind-user scenarios. It is reviewed at every major release.
